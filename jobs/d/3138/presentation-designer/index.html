Front-End developer for Our Website

  • Status: Accepting quotes
  • Job budget: KES 100,000 - 500,000

We’re seeking a talented Frontend Developer with experience in software, travel, and entrepreneurship. Your main mission: convert designs into fast, structured websites.

Your Role:
1. Collaborate with design/product teams to build web pages and Shopify (Liquid) templates.
2. Ensure front-end integration with back-end services.
3. Optimize performance, scalability, and cross-browser compatibility.
4. Implement responsive designs for various devices.
5. Integrate SEO strategies and track web analytics to improve user experience and conversions.
6. Provide technical support and stay updated with trends.

Why Join Us?
1. Work from anywhere.
2. Be part of a growing, ambitious team that values autonomy, well-being, and impact.
3. Flexible hours: contribute value over time worked.
4. Participate in team retreats and be part of a culture of A-players.

Ideal Candidate:
1. 3+ years of experience in web development.
2. Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React or Angular is a plus).
3. Familiar with RESTful APIs, Git, UI/UX principles, and SEO best practices.
4. Fluent in English and French.
5. Our Culture: We thrive on feedback, growth, and critical thinking. If you’re adaptable, motivated, and ready for a fast-paced, startup environment, we want you!

How to Apply:
1. Complete a short questionnaire and attach your CV.
2. Expect a thorough interview process (tech, culture, team, and references).
